The GeoTIFF Web Page

Last Updated: 1 March, 1999

GeoTIFF represents an effort by over 160 different remote sensing, GIS, cartographic, and surveying related companies and organizations to establish a TIFF based interchange format for georeferenced raster imagery.

GeoTIFF Specification

GeoTIFF Revision 1.0

Official Release Version: This is the official version of the GeoTIFF spec, and is the product of substantial review by the GeoTIFF community. This represents Revision 1.0 of the GeoTIFF format.

Information and Support:

The most recent version of the GeoTIFF spec, EPSG/POSC tables, and source code is available via anonymous FTP at the USGS site: 

        ftp://ftpmcmc.cr.usgs.gov/release/geotiff/jpl_mirror/

The USGS also has an archive of prototype GeoTIFF images at:

        ftp://ftpmcmc.cr.usgs.gov/release/geotiff/images/
Information and a hypertext version of the GeoTIFF spec is available via WWW at the following site: 
        http://www.remotesensing.org/geotiff/geotiff.html
The original (now somewhat out of date) GeoTIFF web site is at: 
        http://home.earthlink.net/~ritter/geotiff/geotiff.html

SPOT Image, USA has an additional GeoTIFF Archive at:

	ftp://www.spot.com/ftp/pub/outgoing/GeoTIFF/

Frank Warmerdam maintains some notes, and sample files at:

	http://gdal.velocet.ca/projects/tiff/gt_index.html

There is also now a GeoTIFF FAQ written by Mike Ruth then of Spot Image.

GeoTIFF Mailing List

A mailing-list is currently active to discuss the on-going development of this standard. To subscribe to this list, send e-mail to: 
       listproc@mtmorrison.jpl.nasa.gov
with no subject and the body of the message reading: 
     SUBSCRIBE geotiff  Your_FirstName   Your_LastName
To post inquiries directly to the list, send email to: 
       geotiff@mtmorrison.jpl.nasa.gov

A threaded mail-archive of the GeoTIFF mailing-list discussions is online and available, courtesy of Frank Warmerdam.
