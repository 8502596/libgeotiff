Notes

I get very substantially different results from PROJ.4, and PCI (GCTP)'s results as noted in Random Issues.

The standard parallel does not seem to be widely used, but it is supported by PROJ.4, and is required by the "AUTO" WMS projection 42004. See OGR Bug 257 for details discussing this parameter.

PROJ.4 Organization

  +proj=eqc   +lat_ts=Latitude at projection center 
              +lon_0=Longitude at projection center
              +lon_ts=Latitude of first standard parallel
              +x_0=False Easting
              +y_0=False Northing
